Solomonhills

What is SolomonHills?

SolomonHills is a modern online cooperative platform that simplifies savings and investing and makes it easy and affordable for you to access loans in other to achieve your financial goals. SolomonHills was built to grant everyone access to safe investments.

Why should I join SolomonHills?

With the aid of SolomonHills, a cutting-edge online cooperative platform, you can easily get loans to assist you in reaching your financial objectives while also streamlining your savings and investing strategies. By giving people in Nigeria simple access to the necessary money, Solomonhills secures their future and assists them in achieving their ambitions. SolomonHills is suitable for anyone who wants to
  • have safe place to run to when in need of money.
  • build strong finanacial foundation that secures the future.
  • build wealth for the long-term.
  • invest without the fear of losing money.
  • access risk-free investment instruments.

How do I subscribe?

To start investing on SolomonHills,
  • Visit the website and click on Start for Free”.
  • Input your details and create an account.
  • Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox. Check your spam folder and click on "report not a spam" if you can not find the verification email in your inbox.
  • Complete your KYC information on your dashboard. Input your personal information, next of kin, and bank details. This will enable us to know you better.
  • Click on ‘Cooperative’ if you are accessing the platform with a mobile phone and click on ‘Subscribe’ to complete your subscription while you only need to click on ‘Subscribe’ on the web.

Hills

What is Hills?

Hills is smartly designed to offer you a balance of dividends and growth. It generally protects your funds against inflation and provides growth for your funds in the long term. Hills offers a convenient automatic reinvestment of your funds, with the extra benefit of daily compounding interest.

What is the least amount I can invest in Hills?

The least amount you can invest in Hills is 50,000 Naira.

What is the least amount I can top up with on Hills?

The least amount you can top up with on Hills is 10,000 naira.

When does my investment begin to earn returns?

Your investment (Hills) will begin to earn returns from midnight.

Can I monitor my daily returns?

Yes, you can monitor your daily returns on your dashboard.

What rate of return do I earn on Hills?

Solomonhills offers up to 15% interest per annum on Hills, compounding daily.

What is the minimum investment tenor on Hills?

There is no minimum tenor on Hills.

Bucket

What is Bucket?

Bucket helps you to save for specific goals such as your child’s education, your house rent, a car, and so on. You can easily set up automatic periodic contributions from your bank account by connecting your debit card to help you meet your goal faster.

How many saving goals can I create on Bucket?

You can add as many saving goals as you choose.

What is the least amount I can save on Buckets?

The minimum savings is 200 Naira for the daily and weekly plan, and 500 Naira for the monthly plan.

When do my savings begin to earn returns?

Your savings (Bucket) begin to earn returns from midnight.

Do I have to log in every time I want to top up on Buckets?

Once you make your first savings, your card will be added for automatic periodic contributions and you wouldn’t need to log in every time to top up.

Can I pause and continue automatic saving anytime?

Yes, you can pause and continue automatic saving on your card by enabling or disabling ‘automate’ on your dashboard.

What rate of return do I earn on Buckets?

Solomonhills offers up to 8% interest per annum on Buckets.

Brooks subscription

How do I subscribe?

Login, click on ‘Brooks’ if you are accessing the platform with a mobile phone and click on ‘Subscribe’ to complete your subscription while you only need to click on ‘Subscribe’ on the web.

What is the minimum number of subscriptions that I can make?

The subscription is one-time, with a minimum payment of 500 naira and automatic renewal every month.

Can I increase my subscription amount?

Yes. However, this is only allowed after the initial subscription has been started and is active for six months.

How do I make the payment?

To automate regular monthly contributions, you can only make payments using your debit card. However, anytime a monthly payment is missed owing to an insufficient balance, you can subsequently make the regular payment using your Divi (internal wallet) by clicking on the 'Renew' button

Are there bank charges when you deduct from my account?

No, there are no bank charges on payments made from your card. We bear the cost of all transactions made under your subscription.

Can I monitor my subscription growth?

Yes, you can monitor your monthly subscription growth on your dashboard.

When can I request?

Once your subscription has been active and consistent for at least three months, you are eligible to request a loan.

Are there conditions for requesting a loan?

The only condition is as stated above. However, you can only request a loan of a maximum of X2 of your current total subscription.

What is the interest rate charged on loan?

You just pay 2% interest on the amount borrowed. On the other hand, a free one-month period will be given, after which a 0.4 percent interest charge will be applied to your loan balance every month.

What is Financials?

Financials is simply a way to track your financial records in a smarter way. You can link your bank accounts to see how your money flows at a glance. You will have a quick insight into how you spend and receive your money.

Is this service free?

No, this is a paid service. For the first accounting link, you will be charged NGN 100 , and you will receive a 6-month smart rapid insight into the associated account. The fees are then paid as you proceed according to the list below.
  • Account balance NGN 70.
  • Transactions(Searched by date) NGN 70.

Safety and Security

How secure is my information?

At SolomonHills, safety is our priority and number one core value and so, our platform was built with the security of your data and personal information in mind. Sensitive customer data is encrypted with 256-bit, transmitted on an HTTPS SSL connection, and securely stored.
In addition, we do not store card information as all card payments are processed by our banking and payment partners which are also PCI DSS-compliant and transmitted over a secure network. The PCI DSS is a global standard that stands for "The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)".

How secure is my money?

All saved funds are warehoused and managed by a professional fund manager that is duly registered and licensed by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). We are also a registered cooperative - Solomon Hills Cooperative Multipurpose Society Limited (Registration number, 18079)

Are my card details safe?

We do not store your card details on our database. Your card details are processed by a PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor, Paystack , and are extremely safe.

Are there any management fees?

We use a cooperative approach to conduct our business. A dividend is paid annually and can be withdrawn into your bank account or Divi account (internal wallet). When you need money, you can submit a loan application to be approved without delay. This approach is to secure your funds for the future. After a year of service, you can, however, cancel your subscription for a fee of 3%, which is not advised.

Withdrawals

When can I withdraw my money?

You can withdraw your savings and accrued interest from Buckets at the set maturity date you choose for FREE. A withdrawal button will appear at the maturity of each bucket you create. After the request has been made, funds will be sent to your bank account within 24 hours.
Withdrawal on Hills is made very flexible. You can add money whenever you choose and withdraw once in a month.

What if I withdraw before my set maturity date?

If for any reason, you still choose to withdraw the funds in a bucket before its maturity date, you can request a withdrawal. The selected bucket will be terminated and your funds and accrued interest will be paid to you. However, a penalty of 5% on the value of your bucket will be charged for withdrawals initiated before the set maturity date.
On Hills, withdrawals more than once in a month incur a 5% penalty on the withdrawn amount. This is to help keep you prudent in your investment and grow your funds over time.

How can I withdraw my money?

To initiate a withdrawal,
  • Login to your dashboard.
  • Fill in your bank details, if you haven’t done that earlier.
  • Click on the “withdraw” option on your dashboard.
  • Input your withdrawal amount and choose how you want to receive your money.
  • Confirm withdrawal.
