Introducing Brooks: Retirement Savings with Loan Opportunity

Brooks is a revolutionary retirement savings product that combines the benefits of long-term financial planning with the flexibility of accessing funds when you need them most. With Brooks, you can build your retirement nest egg while having the option to take out a loan against your savings if unforeseen circumstances arise.
Key Features:
Retirement Savings Growth: Brooks is designed to help you grow your retirement savings steadily and securely. By contributing regularly, you can take advantage of compounding interest and market growth to increase your nest egg over time.

Loan Access: One of the unique aspects of Brooks is the option to take out a loan against your accumulated retirement savings. Life can be unpredictable, and emergencies or unexpected expenses may arise. With Brooks, you have the flexibility to access a portion of your retirement funds as a loan, providing you with financial security and peace of mind.

Competitive Interest Rates: When you choose to take a loan against your Brooks retirement savings, you'll benefit from competitive interest rates. This allows you to access the funds you need without the burden of exorbitant borrowing costs.

Flexible Repayment Options: We understand that everyone's financial situation is different. Brooks offers flexible repayment options for your loans, allowing you to tailor the repayment schedule to fit your needs. Whether you prefer shorter-term repayment or a longer-term approach, we provide choices to suit your circumstances.

Retirement Planning Tools: Brooks provides you with comprehensive retirement planning tools and resources to help you make informed decisions. Our platform offers personalized goal setting, investment advice, and insights into your retirement savings progress, ensuring you stay on track to achieve your long-term financial objectives.

Tax Advantages: Similar to traditional retirement savings accounts, Brooks offers potential tax advantages. Depending on your jurisdiction, contributions to your Brooks account may be tax-deductible, and earnings can grow tax-deferred until withdrawal during retirement.

Expert Guidance: Our team of financial experts is dedicated to supporting you on your retirement savings journey. We are here to answer your questions, provide guidance, and offer valuable insights to help you make the most of your retirement savings strategy.

How it works

On the Hills dashboard, click on Invest or Add money. Input the amount you want to invest and select the payment method. There are generally three payment methods- Bank transfer, payment with your debit card, and a voucher sent to you by a loved one.

Your dividend is accrued and added to your investment daily. In fact, your first dividend is paid by midnight after your investment has been approved. Isn't that amazing? Start here.

Withdrawal method

Withdrawal on Hills is made very flexible. It opens once every quarter (every three months) upon maturity of your funds.

Withdrawals of more than one time in a quarter will attract a 6% deduction from the withdrawn amount. This is to help keep you prudent in your savings and grow your funds over time. For an emergency fund with a more flexible withdrawal plan, check out Divi.
