Frequently Asked Questions

Some of answered questions you might love to see

  • How do I subscribe?
    To start investing on SolomonHills,
    • Visit the website and click on Start for Free”.
    • Input your details and create an account.
    • Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox. Check your spam folder and click on "report not a spam" if you can not find the verification email in your inbox.
    • Complete your KYC information on your dashboard. Input your personal information, next of kin, and bank details. This will enable us to know you better.
    • Click on ‘Cooperative’ if you are accessing the platform with a mobile phone and click on ‘Subscribe’ to complete your subscription while you only need to click on ‘Subscribe’ on the web.
  • How do I make payment?

    To automate regular monthly contributions, you can only make payments using your debit card. However, anytime a monthly payment is missed owing to an insufficient balance, you can subsequently make the regular payment using your Divi (internal wallet) by clicking on the 'Renew' button

  • How secure is my information?

    At SolomonHills, safety is our priority and number one core value and so, our platform was built with the security of your data and personal information in mind. Sensitive customer data is encrypted with 256-bit, transmitted on an HTTPS SSL connection, and securely stored.

    In addition, we do not store card information as all card payments are processed by our banking and payment partners which are also PCI DSS-compliant and transmitted over a secure network. The PCI DSS is a global standard that stands for "The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)".

  • Are there any management fees?

    There are no management fees on your savings and investment.
